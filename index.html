BookMark'd

This is a link saver and a work in progress. We hope to launch and add features soon.

BookMark'd allows for people to search through a history of visited pages, not just by title, but by content. It uses stemming to match words like compute and computer, as well as plural and singular forms of a word. The current algorithm used is a simple document similarity based on term frequency and inverse document frequency. We also weight by views and views from a search.
